It’s unclear if Boston forward Jaylen Brown will be in the lineup

Date: NOV 06, 2024

It’s unclear if Boston forward Jaylen Brown will be in the lineup when the Celtics face the visiting Golden State Warriors on Wednesday night. Brown has not played in Boston’s last two games because of a hip flexor strain. Following Monday’s 123-93 victory over Atlanta, Boston coach Joe Mazzulla described Brown as “day to day.”

Jayson Tatum carried the Boston offense against the Hawks by scoring 28 points, 26 of which came in the first half. Tatum has been the leading scorer in six of Boston’s eight games. “He found advantages because he was creating space,” Mazzulla said. “He got a couple in transition. He drove. He had a good balance of shooting threes and driving in the first half, and then they started to hit, and then he was just making plays. He had five assists in the third quarter. Just a well-rounded game by him.
